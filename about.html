Professional Life

With over 15 years in cybersecurity, I've dedicated my career to building resilient security programs that protect organizations while enabling innovation. As a CISO, I lead security strategy with an AI-forward mindset — leveraging machine learning for threat detection, automating security operations, and embedding privacy-by-design principles into everything we build.

My approach to security leadership centers on three pillars: proactive risk management, security culture building, and staying ahead of the curve on emerging threats. I believe the best security programs aren't just defensive — they're strategic business enablers that build trust with customers and stakeholders.

I'm passionate about the convergence of AI and security, particularly in areas like automated threat hunting, AI-driven vulnerability management, and securing AI/ML pipelines against adversarial attacks. I regularly share these insights through writing, podcasts, and conference presentations.

Personal Life

Outside the world of firewalls and threat models, I'm a firm believer that a well-rounded life makes for a better leader. I'm an avid reader — my bookshelves are a mix of cybersecurity textbooks, philosophy, and science fiction. I find that the best ideas often come from connecting dots across completely different disciplines.

I'm a dedicated runner who finds clarity in long-distance training. There's something about pushing through mile 20 of a marathon that mirrors the endurance required in cybersecurity leadership — it's a long game that rewards patience, discipline, and consistent effort.

Family is at the center of everything I do. I'm passionate about mentoring the next generation of security professionals and regularly volunteer my time for cybersecurity education initiatives. I also enjoy cooking, traveling to new places, and exploring the outdoors — activities that remind me there's a beautiful world beyond the screen.

I'm deeply curious about the ethical implications of AI and often find myself in dinner-table debates about technology's role in society. It's this curiosity that drives both my professional work and personal growth.
